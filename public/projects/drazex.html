Created a peer-to-peer logistics/delivery app.

  • This app is primarily built to give users the ability to send and receive packages from one location to another.
  • The app has two main users, the sender and the deliverer.
  • The sender creates a delivery request by specifying the pick-up and drop-off locations, package details, and any special instructions.
  • The deliverer can then browse available delivery requests in their area and choose to accept one that fits their schedule and route.
  • Once a deliverer accepts a request, they can communicate with the sender through in-app messaging to coordinate the pick-up and drop-off details.
  • After the package is delivered, both the sender and deliverer can rate and review each other to help build trust within the community.
  • The app also includes features such as real-time tracking, secure payment processing, and user profiles to enhance the overall experience for both senders and deliverers.
  • Overall, this peer-to-peer logistics/delivery app aims to provide a convenient and reliable way for people to send and receive packages while also creating opportunities for individuals to earn money by becoming deliverers.
  • The app is built with Flutter, a popular cross-platform mobile app development framework, which allows for a seamless user experience across both iOS and Android devices.
  • The app also utilizes various APIs and services, such as Google Maps for location tracking and payment gateways for secure transactions.
